# 🛡️ Security Policy

## 🛡️ Responsible Disclosure Policy

At **Artifact-Shield**, we take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

---

## 📅 Reporting a Vulnerability

**Do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.** 

Please report all vulnerabilities by following these steps:
1.  **Email Us**: Send a detailed report to `security@dhoondlay.io` (Replace with your actual security email).
2.  **Report Contents**:
    *   **Description**: A clear summary of the vulnerability.
    *   **Reproducibility**: Step-by-step instructions or PoC code.
    *   **Impact**: How this affects the gateway or downstream LLMs.
3.  **Encrypted Communcation**: If you'd like to use a GPG key for encryption, request it in your initial email.

---

## ⌛ Our Commitment to You

-   **Initial Response**: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **48 hours**.
-   **Triage & Resolution**: We will provide a status update every **5 business days** until the issue is resolved.
-   **Coordinated Disclosure**: We aim to resolve all high-severity vulnerabilities within **14 days** and will coordinate a public disclosure date with you.

---

## 🏆 Bug Bounty

Artifact-Shield is currently a community-driven project. While we do not offer monetary bug bounties at this time, we will **publicly credit** all researchers who discover significant vulnerabilities in our release notes and Hall of Fame.

---
*Thank you for helping us keep Enterprise AI secure and private!*
